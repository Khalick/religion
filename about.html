Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a new religion? ‚ñº

No. The Unified Path is not a religion but a spiritual philosophy and practice. We encourage people to deepen their existing faith traditions while also learning from the universal wisdom found across all paths. Think of it as a bridge between traditions, not a replacement for them.

Do I need to give up my current beliefs? ‚ñº

Absolutely not. Many of our community members are practicing Christians, Muslims, Buddhists, Hindus, and more. The Unified Path complements your existing practice by offering additional perspectives and tools. You remain true to your own tradition while appreciating the wisdom in others.

What if I'm new to spirituality? ‚ñº
